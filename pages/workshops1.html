Quantum Programming with Classiq

This workshop is brought to you by Quantum Club at University of Calgary in collaboration with Classiq, supported by Quantum City and Mphasis. The workshop is open to senior undergraduates, graduate students (MSc and PhD), postdocs, and faculty members at University of Calgary only.

Dates & Time

  • Dates: Tue Sep 23 – Thu Sep 25, 2025
  • Time: 12–2 PM MDT (Calgary)
  • Location: In-person (qHub) & Online (Zoom)

Schedule

  1. Day 1: Introduction to Quantum Programming with Classiq
  2. Day 2: Quantum Oracles and Uses
  3. Day 3: Use Cases and Algorithms in the Financial Sector

Why Join?

  • Hands-on training and skill development in quantum programming.
  • Certificate of Participation for attending all 3 days.
  • Exclusive opportunity to join open projects with Classiq after the workshop.
  • Project teams selected based on workshop performance.

Prerequisites

  • Basic knowledge of quantum computing and Python programming is required.

Software Requirements

  • Jupyter Notebooks (run locally with VSCode, Anaconda, or PyCharm) or online (Google Colab).
