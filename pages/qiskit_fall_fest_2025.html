Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 is here!

The Qiskit Fall Fest is a global series of quantum computing events hosted by student-led organizations and supported by IBM Quantum. At University of Calgary Quantum Club, our Fall Fest brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals to learn, collaborate, and innovate with Qiskit and quantum technologies.

Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 events organized by University of Calgary Quantum Club are open to undergraduates, graduates (MSc and PhD), postdocs, and faculty members at University of Calgary.

Students from other universities in Canada are also welcome to participate and should contact us for details (see "Contact Us" section below).
