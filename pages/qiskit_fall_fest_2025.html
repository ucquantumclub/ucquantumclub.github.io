Fall Fest 2025 is here!

The Qiskit Fall Fest is a global series of quantum computing events hosted by student-led organizations and supported by IBM Quantum. At University of Calgary Quantum Club, our Fall Fest brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals to learn, collaborate, and innovate with Qiskit and quantum technologies.
